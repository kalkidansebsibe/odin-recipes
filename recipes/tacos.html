Turkey and Yam Spicy Tacos

This turkey and yam spicy tacos recipe is a delicious and healthy twist on traditional tacos. The combination of ground turkey and sweet potatoes creates a flavorful filling that is sure to please everyone. Serve with your favorite toppings for a fun and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Your favorite taco toppings (lettuce, cheese, salsa, etc.)

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground turkey over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the diced sweet potato, onion, and garlic to the skillet. Cook until the sweet potato is tender.
  3. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Serve the turkey and yam mixture in taco shells or tortillas with your favorite toppings.
