Simple Beef Short Ribs

This simple beef short ribs recipe is a delicious and hearty dish that is perfect for a cozy dinner. The ribs are slow-cooked to perfection, resulting in tender meat that falls off the bone. Serve with mashed potatoes or crusty bread to soak up the flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef short ribs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme) for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. In a large oven-safe pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the short ribs and brown on all sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot and sauté until softened.
  4. Return the short ribs to the pot and add the beef broth, red wine, tomato paste, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover the pot and transfer it to the preheated oven. Cook for 2-3 hours, or until the meat is tender and falls off the bone.
  6. Remove from the oven and let rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
