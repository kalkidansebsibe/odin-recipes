Easy Cheesy Lasagna

This easy cheesy lasagna recipe is a simple lasagna that takes about half the effort of traditional versions. I always make extra for leftovers, but the more I cook, the more they eat! Serve with garlic toast and salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 2 (15 ounce) containers ricotta cheese
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef and sausage. Drain off excess fat.
  3. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese, and 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with 1/3 of the noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, and 1/3 of the remaining meat sauce. Repeat layers twice more, ending with meat sauce. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
